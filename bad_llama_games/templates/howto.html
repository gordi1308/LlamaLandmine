How to Play

  1. Pick your level - Easy, Normal, Hard

  2. Click on a square in the grid:
    If it's a Mine - GAME OVER
    If it's a Llama - WOOP you found one!
    Otherwise it'll have two numbers, they tell you the
    amount of Llamas and Mines in the surrounding squares

  3. REPEAT TILL YOU WIN OR DIE TRYING!

  4. May the odds forever be in your favour...

Tip !

Right-Click to flag
the square as a Mine!
