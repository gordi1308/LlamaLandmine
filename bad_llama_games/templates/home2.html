About the game

class="text-body"> It's 2016 and war has broken out across Europe. The death toll has already surpassed 1 million and is rising by the day. Portuguese Rebels have launched an invasion into Spain and the Spanish defenses are crumbling under the relentless assault. Germany insurrections have overthrown the government and Swedish cartels now control most of Scandinavia. With hopes of peace and justice fading fast, the continent finds itself crying out for a hero to save them from total destruction... Meanwhile a herd of Peruvian Llamas have wandered into a nuclear minefield!
